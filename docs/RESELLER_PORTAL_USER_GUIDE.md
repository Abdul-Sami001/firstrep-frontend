# Reseller Portal – Quick Guide (Non-Technical)

This guide explains how a reseller logs in, what they can do, and the benefits of using the portal.

## How to Access
1. Go to `/ResellerLogin`.
2. Enter your business email and password, then click **Sign In to Portal**.
3. On success you are redirected to `/ResellerDashboard`.
4. If you see an access error, your reseller account may be pending approval—contact the reseller team.

## What You Can Do
- **Dashboard Overview**
  - See your current tier and status.
  - View sales and commission metrics (lifetime and recent periods).
  - See top-performing storefronts.
- **Commissions**
  - Browse all commission entries with status (pending/earned/paid/voided).
  - Filter by status or storefront, and page through the ledger.
  - See quick summaries for this month and the last 30 days.
- **Storefronts**
  - View your storefronts, their type (online/screen/link), and activation state.
  - Inspect products assigned to a storefront.
- **Marketing Assets**
  - Access approved assets (images, PDFs, copy) for your tier.
  - Open or download assets for campaigns, screens, or social posts.
- **Profile**
  - Update company info, website, contact name/email/phone, VAT, and payout details.

## Benefits for Resellers
- Transparent commission tracking with clear statuses and payout context.
- Tier-based access to marketing materials to promote faster.
- Multiple storefront support so you can track performance by location/link/screen.
- Self-service profile updates to keep payout and contact details current.

## Tips & Best Practices
- Keep your contact email/phone and payout details up to date to avoid payout delays.
- Use storefront-specific links/QRs so attribution stays accurate.
- Check the commission ledger regularly to confirm paid/earned amounts.
- Download the latest marketing assets before campaigns to ensure brand consistency.

## Getting Help
- If you cannot log in or your account shows as pending/suspended, reach out to the reseller team.
- For missing assets or storefront corrections, contact support with your storefront slug and issue details.
